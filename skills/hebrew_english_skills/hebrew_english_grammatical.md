# Grammatical/Syntactic Awareness: Hebrew to English

## Major Transformations

### 1. Definiteness System
**Hebrew:** ה- prefix
**English:** "the" or "a/an" as separate words
- הספר → the book
- ספר → a book

### 2. Adjective Positioning
**Hebrew:** After noun, with ה if noun definite
**English:** Before noun, no agreement
- הבית הלבן → the white house
- בית לבן → a white house

### 3. Preposition Expansion
**Hebrew:** Prefixes ב, ל, מ
**English:** Separate words
- בבית → in the house
- לבית → to the house
- מהבית → from the house

### 4. Object Marker Removal
**Hebrew:** את before definite objects
**English:** No marker
- רואה את הספר → see the book

### 5. Verb Simplification
**Hebrew:** Seven binyanim
**English:** Simple verbs (+ auxiliaries)
- נכתב (nikhtav - Nif'al) → was written

### 6. Gender to Pronoun
**Hebrew gender → English pronouns:**
- הוא/היא → he/she
- Only 3rd person in English

### 7. Negation Structure
**Hebrew:** לא before verb
**English:** not after auxiliary, or don't/doesn't
- לא מדבר → doesn't speak / don't speak

### 8. Question Formation
**Hebrew:** Intonation or האם
**English:** Auxiliary inversion
- ?אתה הולך → Are you going?

### 9. Relative Clauses
**Hebrew:** ש- prefix
**English:** that/which/who (separate words)
- הספר שקראתי → the book that I read

### 10. Subject Addition
**Hebrew:** Can omit subjects
**English:** Requires subjects
- "הולך לעבודה" → "I am going to work" (add subject)

### 11. Progressive Tenses
**Hebrew:** Present tense serves both
**English:** May need progressive
- אני אוכל → I eat / I am eating

### 12. Plural Handling
**Hebrew:** -im/-ot + dual
**English:** -s/-es, no dual
- ידיים (dual) → two hands

### 13. Possession Conversion
**Hebrew:** Suffixes or של
**English:** 's or possessive adjectives
- ספרי → my book
- הספר שלי → my book
- ספר יוחנן → John's book

### 14. Passive Reduction
**Hebrew:** Passive binyanim very common
**English:** Active often more natural
- נכתב → was written (keep passive) or "someone wrote" (active)

## Transformation Checklist
- [ ] Convert ה- to "the" or add "a/an"
- [ ] Move adjectives before nouns
- [ ] Expand preposition prefixes
- [ ] Remove את object markers
- [ ] Determine binyan meaning
- [ ] Add articles where needed
- [ ] Choose pronouns from gender
- [ ] Add subjects if omitted
- [ ] Convert relative ש- to English relative pronouns
- [ ] Structure negation with auxiliaries
- [ ] Add auxiliary verbs for questions
- [ ] Simplify binyanim to English verbs
- [ ] Handle progressive as needed
- [ ] Convert possessive structures
